PrivateClaw creates a private, end-to-end encrypted path between a user and a self-hosted or cloud OpenClaw deployment.

Use a one-time QR invite to join a session, keep the relay blind to message contents, and move sensitive conversations away from public chat surfaces.

Features:
- Scan or paste one-time invite links
- End-to-end encrypted relay transport
- Works with custom home or cloud relays
- Designed for private OpenClaw conversations
- Lightweight session flow for secure access
